About

My usual routine starts with waking up at 7:50. I get up and make my bed first. Then, I read a book with information I find useful for life for about 40 minutes and write down my thoughts. After that, I spend 20 minutes memorizing English vocabulary and solving daily listening and reading exercises.

At 8:50, I prepare breakfast. First, I microwave the rice for 2 minutes and 30 seconds. At the same time, I heat some oil in an electric pan. Once it's hot enough, I crack two eggs into the pan to make fried eggs. When the rice is done, I take it out of the microwave, open a can of tuna, and place it next to the rice. Then, I add some mayonnaise to the tuna and mix it together. Afterward, I place the fried eggs on top of the rice. I enjoy this meal with kimchi and some morning juice.

After breakfast, I take various supplements: magnesium, lutein, multivitamins, and milk thistle. Then, I wash the dishes and get ready for the day. I wash my face, shave, wash and dry my hair, change clothes, and pack my laptop and water bottle in my bag. I usually leave the house around 9:40. After attending my first and second classes, I return home around 1:30.

At 1:30, I eat a banana and a tangerine for lunch. I then rest or do homework until 3:00. At 3:00, I head to the gym for my workout. I divide my workouts into four sessions throughout the week: chest and triceps, back and quadriceps, lower body, and shoulders. After working out for about one to two hours, I return home and prepare dinner right away. My dinner is the same every day: teriyaki chicken breast marinated in honey garlic sauce and stir-fried with sliced onions. For sides, I have a vegetable salad with Caesar dressing, rice, and kimchi.

After dinner, I wash the dishes and take a shower. Feeling refreshed, I begin the second part of my day, which consists of doing homework. I usually spend about four hours, from 6 to 10, studying or completing assignments. Once I finish by 10, I have about an hour of free time before bed. During this time, I do things I wanted to do during the day, such as watching YouTube, relaxing, and unwinding before going to sleep.
